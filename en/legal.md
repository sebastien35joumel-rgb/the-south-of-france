## Personal data

Données personnelles · RGPD

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU 2016/679) and the French _Loi Informatique et Libertés_, we collect personal data only when you choose to give it to us:

-   **The Letter** — when you subscribe, we store your email address for the sole purpose of sending the monthly letter. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in each email.
-   **Contact** — the name, email and message you send through the contact form are used only to answer you, and kept no longer than necessary.

Data is never sold or shared with third parties for advertising. You have the right to access, rectify, delete, restrict and port your data, and to object to its processing. To exercise these rights, use our [contact form](https://the-south-of-france.com/en/contact/) or write to the registered office above.

You may also lodge a complaint with the [CNIL](https://www.cnil.fr), the French data protection authority.

Data controller: **NEWP**.

## Cookies

Cookies et mesure d’audience

This site keeps cookies to a minimum. We use no advertising trackers and no third-party social cookies.

-   **Essential cookies** — required for the site to function (language preference, form security). These do not require consent.
-   **Audience measurement** — we currently use no audience-measurement cookies. Should we add a privacy-first, cookieless analytics tool, this notice will be updated beforehand.

You can refuse or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.
